About the school

The School on Group Actions will take place in Montevideo, Uruguay, from October 5 to October 9, 2026.

The aim of the school is to bring together students, early-career researchers, and specialists working on group actions and related areas, including one-dimensional dynamics, symbolic dynamics, computability aspects of dynamical systems, and geometric group theory.

The program will include lecture courses, research talks, and informal discussion sessions. Further details will be posted on this page.
